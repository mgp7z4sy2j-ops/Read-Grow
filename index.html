Simple process

From curiosity
to knowledge in 4 steps.

Our streamlined process makes it easy to discover, request, and receive books that accelerate your professional growth.

01

Describe your challenge

Tell our AI about a work problem you're facing or a skill you want to develop. No format required — just write naturally.

02

Get AI recommendations

Receive 5 personalized books ranked by relevance, each with a clear explanation of why it matches your situation and what problem it solves.

03

Select your book

Choose the book that resonates most. Confirm your delivery address and reading goal. HR handles approval, purchasing, and shipping.

04

Read, reflect, review

Your book arrives in STARTRADER packaging. After reading, share a short review. Your insights help colleagues discover great reads too.

Everything you need
to know.

All active employees with a @startrader.com or @starprime.com email address are eligible. Email domain is verified at registration — no other domains are accepted. Contractors and external partners are not eligible at this time.

Each quarter (Q1–Q4) you receive a $80 USD company-funded allowance for one book. Shipping is covered separately — it doesn't count against your budget. Unused quota expires at the end of the quarter and cannot be rolled over. The annual total is $320 per employee.

Any book from our curated catalogue of ~2,000 titles. The catalogue covers leadership, sales, technology, product, personal development, creativity, wellness, and more. Our AI will recommend the most relevant titles from your description — you're not limited to a genre.

Once HR approves your order (typically within 1–2 business days), the book is purchased and packaged. Delivery usually takes 3–7 business days depending on your location. You'll receive a tracking notification when your book ships.

Books priced above $80 are excluded from AI recommendations by default. For special cases (e.g. technical textbooks or reference volumes slightly over budget), HR has override capability and can approve on a case-by-case basis. Contact your HR team to discuss.

No — reviews are strongly encouraged but never required. The program's only ask is a short review when you're done. Your review helps colleagues discover great reads and improves AI recommendations for everyone. Reviews are visible to all company employees in the Community feed.
